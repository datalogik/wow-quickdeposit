# QuickDeposit

A World of Warcraft addon that adds quick deposit and withdraw buttons to the Warband Bank.

## Features

- Quick deposit/withdraw panel that appears below the bank frame when you open your Warband Bank
- Fixed amounts: 5g, 10g, 50g, 100g, 500g, 1,000g, 10,000g
- Percentage-based: 25%, 50%, 75%, 100% of your current gold or bank balance
- Currency breakdown: deposit/withdraw just your gold, silver, or copper separately
- Buttons automatically disabled when you don't have enough gold for that action
- Button states refresh whenever your money changes

## Usage

1. Visit any banker and open your Warband Bank tab.
2. The QuickDeposit panel appears below the bank frame with two sections:
   - **Deposit** (left) - transfer gold from your character to the Warband Bank
   - **Withdraw** (right) - transfer gold from the Warband Bank to your character
3. Click any button to instantly transfer that amount.

## Installation

Copy the `QuickDeposit` folder into your `World of Warcraft/_retail_/Interface/AddOns/` directory.
